About Me

My name is Liam Meagher. I'm an engineer with an Undergraduate Degree in Computer Science & Engineering from UCLA with the goal of pursuing a Master's Degree in Mechanical Engineering. I have a background in Combat Robotics and Additive Manufacturing and am always learning new skills! I achieved the rank of Eagle Scout in Scouts BSA on May 16th, 2019. I spent my summers from 2017 to 2023 volunteering at Camp Emerald Bay, a Scout camp on Catalina Island, as a counselor and instructor. This experience taught me self-discipline and leadership skills, and I returned to the camp as the director of my previous program once I turned 18. I am a member of the American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME) and I have a passion for teaching and mentoring youth in STEM and attention to detail.
